Lexoris — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 November 2025 Contact: admin@lexoris.com.au

1. Overview Lexoris respects your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. What We Collect We collect only the information you provide: - First Name and Last Name - Email Address - Workplace or Firm Name Transcription audio and text are processed temporarily and deleted after download.

3. Purpose of Collection We use this information to: - Create and manage your account - Provide and improve our transcription service - Process payments through Stripe - Communicate service updates or support matters We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Storage and Security All data are hosted on secure Australian servers. Lexoris uses encryption and access controls to protect your information. Transcripts and audio files are automatically deleted after download or within a short retention period for technical recovery (if applicable).

5. Third-Party Services Lexoris uses: - Stripe for secure payments (subject to Stripe’s own Privacy Policy) - AI transcription providers hosted in Australia for processing only (data are not used to train models). We take reasonable steps to ensure all service providers comply with Australian privacy standards.

6. Access and Correction You may request access to or correction of your personal information at any time by emailing admin@lexoris.com.au.

7. Retention and Deletion Personal account data are retained while your account is active and deleted once no longer required for billing or legal purposes.

8. Complaints If you believe we have breached your privacy: - Email admin@lexoris.com.au outlining your concern. - We will respond within 30 days. If unresolved, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

9. Updates This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.
